Our story

Built around a flame.

Placeholder — your friend's real story goes here

Firepit started with one obsession: pizza cooked properly, over real wood flame. Years of dough experiments and feeding friends from a tiny garden oven turned into a mobile pizzeria built to bring fire-blistered pizza to the streets.

Every dough is slow-proved for 48 hours, topped with the best we can find, and cooked in front of you in 90 seconds. No shortcuts — just proper pizza and a bit of fire-side theatre.
